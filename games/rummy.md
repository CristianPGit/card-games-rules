## рҹҺ® How to Play

Each turn has **3 steps**:

1. **Draw** вҖ” take one card from the stock OR the top of the discard pile.
2. **Meld (optional)** вҖ” lay down any valid set/run, or add cards to existing melds (yours or others, depending on variant).
3. **Discard** вҖ” place one card face-up on the discard pile. Turn ends.

When a player has melded all their cards (with one card to discard last), they **"go out"** and win the round.

## рҹҸҶ Scoring

When someone goes out, opponents score penalty points for cards left in hand:

| Card | Points |
|:----:|:------:|
| 2вҖ“10 | Face value |
| J / Q / K | 10 each |
| A | 1 (low) or 15 (high) вҖ” agree first |

Game often ends when someone reaches **100 or 500 points** (loser high) вҖ” adjust to taste.

## рҹ’Ў Strategy Tips

- рҹҺҜ **Plan two melds at once** вҖ” keep cards that could go either way.
- рҹ—‘пёҸ **Watch the discard pile** вҖ” never feed opponents the card they're collecting.
- вҡЎ **Go out fast in early rounds** вҖ” penalty points add up.
- рҹғҸ **Don't hoard high cards** (J/Q/K) вҖ” they're 10 points each if caught.
