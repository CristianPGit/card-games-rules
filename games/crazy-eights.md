# 8️⃣ Crazy Eights

> The original UNO. Match suit or rank, dump your hand, and use 8s as wild cards.

<div align="center">

| 👥 Players | 🃏 Deck | ⏱️ Time | ⭐ Difficulty |
|:----------:|:------:|:------:|:------------:|
| 2–7 | 52 cards | 15–25 min | Easy |

</div>

---

## 🎯 Goal

**Be the first to get rid of all your cards.**

---

## 🃏 Setup

| Players | Cards each |
|:-------:|:----------:|
| 2 | 7 |
| 3+ | 5 |

1. Deal as above.
2. Place rest face-down as the **stock**.
3. Flip top card to start the **discard pile**.
   - If it's an 8, bury it and flip another.

---

## 🎮 How to Play

On your turn, play **one card** that matches the top of the discard pile by:

- ✅ Same **suit**, or
- ✅ Same **rank**, or
- ✅ **Any 8** (wild — see below)

Can't play? **Draw from stock** until you can (some house rules: just draw 1).

---

## ⭐ The Crazy 8

When you play an 8, **declare a new suit**. The next player must play that suit (or another 8).

---

## ⚡ Optional Special Cards

These vary by house — agree first!

| Card | Effect |
|:----:|--------|
| **2** | Next player draws 2 (stacks) |
| **Q♠** | Next player draws 5 |
| **J** | Skip next player |
| **A** | Reverse direction |

---

## 🏆 Scoring

When someone goes out, others count points in hand:

| Card | Points |
|:----:|:------:|
| 8 | 50 |
| K, Q, J, 10 | 10 |
| A | 1 |
| Others | Face value |

First to reach 100 points (loser) ends the game. Lowest total wins.

---

## 💡 Strategy Tips

- 🎯 **Save your 8s for tight spots** — wilds are most valuable late.
- 👀 **Track the suit pattern** — note what opponents avoid.
- 🃏 **Dump high-value cards first** in case you don't go out.

---

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes

- ❌ Playing an 8 without declaring a suit
- ❌ Holding 8s when you could've dumped them
