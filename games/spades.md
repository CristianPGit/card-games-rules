# ♣️ Spades

> A team-based trick-taking game built around **bidding**. Promise tricks, deliver them, win big.

<div align="center">

| 👥 Players | 🃏 Deck | ⏱️ Time | ⭐ Difficulty |
|:----------:|:------:|:------:|:------------:|
| 4 (2 teams of 2) | 52 cards | 30–60 min | Hard |

</div>

---

## 🎯 Goal

**First team to 500 points wins.** Spades are always trump. ♠️

---

## 🃏 Setup

1. Partners sit **across** from each other.
2. Deal all 52 cards — **13 each**.
3. Cards rank: **A (high) → K → Q → J → 10 → ... → 2 (low)**.
4. ♠️ Spades **always beat** every other suit.

---

## 🎙️ Bidding (the key skill)

Before play, each player declares **how many tricks they'll take** (0 through 13).

- A team's bid = sum of both partners' bids.
- A **bid of 0 = "Nil"** — partner risks more for big bonus (see below).

---

## 🎮 How to Play

1. Player left of dealer leads any non-Spade card (Spades are "not broken" yet).
2. Going clockwise, each player **must follow suit** if able.
3. Can't follow? Play any card — **including a Spade to "trump."**
4. Highest Spade wins. If no Spades played, highest card of led suit wins.
5. Winner leads the next trick.

> 🚫 You can't lead Spades until someone has trumped a trick (Spades are "broken").

---

## 🏆 Scoring

After all 13 tricks, score each team:

### ✅ Made your bid
- **10 × bid** points, plus **1 point per overtrick** ("bag").

### ❌ Missed your bid
- **−10 × bid** points (negative!).

### 🛍️ Bags Penalty
Every 10 bags accumulated = **−100 points**. So overtricks hurt long-term!

### 🌟 Nil Bonus
- Successful Nil (took 0 tricks): **+100**
- Failed Nil (took any trick): **−100**

---

## 🌙 Blind Nil (Advanced)

Bid Nil **before looking at your cards** — for **±200 points**. High-risk classic.

---

## 💡 Strategy Tips

- 🎯 **Count tricks carefully** — overbidding tanks you, underbidding builds bags.
- 🤝 **Read your partner's bid** — adjust yours to balance.
- ♠️ **Save spades for stealing tricks** opponents lead.
- 🚮 **Dump high cards** when partner is winning a trick — don't waste them.

---

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes

- ❌ Overbidding to "look good" — penalty is harsh
- ❌ Forgetting bags accumulate across rounds
- ❌ Leading spades too early — illegal until broken
