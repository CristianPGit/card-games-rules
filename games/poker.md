# ♦️ Poker — Texas Hold'em

> The world's most popular poker variant. Two hole cards, five community cards, four betting rounds. Easy to learn, lifetime to master.

| 👥 Players | 🃏 Deck | ⏱️ Time | ⭐ Difficulty |

## 🎯 Goal

**Make the best 5-card hand**, or bluff everyone else into folding.

---

## 🏆 Hand Rankings (Best → Worst)

| Rank | Hand | Example |
|:----:|------|---------|
| 1 | 👑 **Royal Flush** | 10-J-Q-K-A all same suit |
| 2 | 🌊 **Straight Flush** | 5 in a row, same suit |
| 3 | 🎰 **Four of a Kind** | 4× same rank |
| 4 | 🏠 **Full House** | 3 of a kind + a pair |
| 5 | 💧 **Flush** | 5 cards same suit |
| 6 | 📏 **Straight** | 5 in a row, mixed suits |
| 7 | 🎯 **Three of a Kind** | 3× same rank |
| 8 | ✌️✌️ **Two Pair** | Two different pairs |
| 9 | ✌️ **One Pair** | 2× same rank |
| 10 | 🔝 **High Card** | Highest single card |

> 🃏 Ties broken by **kickers** (next highest card).

---

## 🃏 Setup

1. One player has the **Dealer button** (rotates clockwise each hand).
2. Player left of dealer posts **small blind** (forced bet).
3. Next player posts **big blind** (2× small blind).
4. Each player gets **2 cards face-down** ("hole cards").

---

## 🎮 The Four Betting Rounds

### 1️⃣ Pre-Flop
After hole cards are dealt. Starting left of big blind, each player can:
- **Fold** — quit the hand
- **Call** — match the current bet
- **Raise** — increase the bet

### 2️⃣ The Flop
Dealer puts **3 community cards face-up** in the middle. New betting round.

### 3️⃣ The Turn
Dealer adds a **4th community card**. New betting round.

### 4️⃣ The River
Dealer adds a **5th and final community card**. Final betting round.

---

## 🏁 The Showdown

If 2+ players remain, they reveal cards. **Best 5-card hand** (made from any combination of the 2 hole + 5 community cards) wins the pot.

If only one player remains (everyone else folded), they win — and don't have to show their cards.

```
   Your hole cards:      [A♠] [K♠]
   Community cards:  [Q♠][J♠][10♠][3♦][7♣]
                     └─────────────────┘
   Your best 5 cards:  A♠ K♠ Q♠ J♠ 10♠  = ROYAL FLUSH 👑
```

---

## 💡 Strategy Tips

- 🎯 **Play tight early** — only strong starting hands (pairs, A-K, A-Q, suited connectors).
- 👀 **Position matters** — acting last gives you info on others.
- 🧮 **Pot odds** — calculate if a call is worth it vs. the pot size.
- 🃏 **Bluff sparingly** — works best against thoughtful opponents.
- 😶 **Don't tilt** — bad beats happen; play the math, not the emotion.

## 🎲 Starting Hand Strength (rough guide)

| Tier | Hands |
|:----:|-------|
| 🔥 Premium | AA, KK, QQ, AK |
| 💪 Strong | JJ, 10-10, AQ, AJ, KQ |
| 👍 Playable | 99–22, suited connectors (JT, T9), Ax suited |
| 😬 Marginal | Everything else — fold most |

---

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes

- ❌ Playing too many hands ("any 2 cards can win" — they can, but rarely)
- ❌ Calling instead of raising with strong hands
- ❌ Bluffing players who always call
- ❌ Chasing draws with bad pot odds

---

## 🌍 Other Poker Variants

- **Omaha** — 4 hole cards, must use exactly 2
- **Seven-Card Stud** — no community cards, 7 cards each
- **Five-Card Draw** — classic home-game poker, draw to replace cards
